Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. 1 cup of milk powder
  2. 1/4 cup of all-purpose flour (maida)
  3. 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda
  4. A pinch of cardamom powder (optional, for flavor)
  5. 2 tablespoons of ghee (clarified butter) or unsalted butter
  6. 2-3 tablespoons of milk (approximately) to knead the dough

Recipe
